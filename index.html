Simple Omelette Recipe

An easy and Quick dish,perfect for any meal.This classic omelette combines beaten eggs cooked to perfection,optionallly filled with your choice of cheese, vegetables, or meats.

Preparation Time

  • Total : Approximately 10 min
  • Preparation : 5 min
  • Cooking : 5 min

Ingredients

  • 2-3 large egg
  • Salt, to tast
  • Pepper, to tast
  • 1 table spoon of butter or oil
  • Optional filling: Cheese, diced vegetables, meat, or herbs

Instruction

  1. Beat the eggs: In a bowl, beat the eggs with a pinch of salt and pepper untilthey are well mixed. You can add a tablespoone of water or milk for the fluffier texture
  2. Heat the pan: Place a non-stick fry pan over a medium heat and add butter or oil
  3. Cook the oelette: one the butter is melted and budding, pour in the eggs. Tilt the pan to ensure the eggs evenly coat ont the surface
  4. Add filling (optional): When the eggs begin to set at the edges but are still slightly runny in the middle, sprinkle your choose filling over one half of the omelette
  5. Fold and Serve: As the omelette continues to cook, carefully lift one edge and fold it over the filling. Let it cook for another min, then slide on to a plate.
  6. Enjoy: Serve hot, with additional salt and pepper
